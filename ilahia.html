ILAHIA COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING AND TECHNOLOGY

Ilahia College of Engineering and Technology (ICET), Mulavoor P.O., Muvattupuzha is a co-educational minority institution approved by AICTE, New Delhi and affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Kerala Technological University (KTU). The college is established in the year 2002.
